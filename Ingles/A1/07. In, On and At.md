Time:

- At: For hours and specific parts of the day.
	- At 6:00am.
	- At noon / night / midnight.
- In: For years, centuries or some parts of the day.
	- In 1969
	- In the 15th century
	- In the morning / afternoon / evening.
- On: For months, days of the week and parts of the week.
	- On January.
	- On Saturday.
	- On the weekend.
	- On weekdays.
